Why Coral Gables?

Coral Gables is Miami's most beautiful and architecturally significant neighborhood, designed by George Merrick in the 1920s. Known as the "City Beautiful," it offers a unique blend of historic charm and modern luxury.

Lifestyle & Amenities

  • Miracle Mile: Premier shopping district with luxury boutiques and restaurants
  • University of Miami: World-class research institution and cultural hub
  • Gables Estates: Exclusive gated communities with resort-style amenities
  • Venetian Pool: Historic swimming hole and botanical gardens
  • Golf & Tennis: Championship courses and private clubs

Investment Potential

Coral Gables' architectural restrictions and limited development preserve its value. The neighborhood's proximity to downtown Miami, excellent schools, and resort-like amenities make it a premium investment choice.
