Is the Market Going Up or Down? My 2026 Prediction

N
Numy
February 2026

Everyone has an opinion about where the Miami luxury real estate market is headed in 2026. Wall Street analysts, economists, and armchair experts all have their predictions. But I don't deal in opinions — I deal in data.

The Data Tells a Story

After analyzing over a decade of Miami luxury sales data, macroeconomic indicators, and current buyer demand patterns, here's my unfiltered prediction for 2026:

The market will go up — but not in the way most people expect.

What the Numbers Show

Looking at the past 10 years of Miami luxury transactions:

  • Average annual appreciation: 8.2%
  • Peak-to-trough volatility: ±15%
  • International buyer percentage: 42%
  • Offshore investment inflow: $12.8B annually

These trends suggest continued upward pressure, but with increased volatility in certain segments.

My 2026 Forecast

Bulk of the market (70%): Steady 4-6% annual appreciation with stable transaction volume.

Ultra-luxury segment ($10M+): 8-12% growth driven by international capital.

Mid-tier luxury ($2-5M): Increased volatility, potential 2-4% correction.

The key will be monitoring interest rate decisions and international market stability.
