Why Fisher Island?

Fisher Island is Miami's most exclusive address, a private island accessible only by ferry or private boat. This billionaires' playground offers unmatched privacy, security, and luxury amenities.

Lifestyle & Amenities

  • Private Island: Exclusive access via ferry or private boat only
  • The Grove: World-class clubhouse with fine dining and spa
  • Championship Golf: Private 18-hole course designed by P.B. Dye
  • Marina & Yacht Club: Full-service marina with yacht charters
  • 24/7 Security: Comprehensive security and concierge services

Investment Potential

Fisher Island's limited inventory (only 800 lots) and private island status create exceptional scarcity. The island's prestige as a global elite destination ensures premium pricing and consistent appreciation.
