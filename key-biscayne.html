Why Key Biscayne?

Key Biscayne is Miami's tropical island escape, offering pristine beaches, championship golf, and resort-style living just minutes from downtown Miami. This family-friendly paradise combines natural beauty with urban convenience.

Lifestyle & Amenities

  • Crandon Park: 1,000-acre beachfront park with marina and nature trails
  • Bill Baggs Cape Florida State Park: Historic lighthouse and pristine beaches
  • Crandon Golf Club: Championship golf course with ocean views
  • Tennis Center: World-class tennis facilities and programs
  • Island Living: Bridge access with security and privacy

Investment Potential

Key Biscayne's limited land area and protected status create natural scarcity. The island's proximity to Miami's business districts, combined with its resort amenities, makes it an attractive investment for both primary and second home buyers.
